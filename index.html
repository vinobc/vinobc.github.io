About

Dr. Vinob Chander R is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Assistant Director i/c (Academics) at Amity University Bengaluru. He holds a Ph.D. in IoT from Anna University, Chennai, where his doctoral research addressed application interoperability for the Internet of Things. His M.E. in Computer Science and Engineering (First Class with Distinction) is from Vinayaka Missions Research Foundation, and his B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering from Gulbarga University.

With over 21 years of professional experience, Dr. Chander has built a career that bridges academia and industry. He has held faculty positions at SSN College of Engineering (Chennai), Parul University (Gujarat), and SNIST (Hyderabad), contributing to teaching, research, curriculum development, and institutional administration. In industry, he served as a Senior Manager at HCL Technologies, designing corporate training programs and developing technical solutions for clients including Robert Bosch and Sony.

As Assistant Director i/c (Academics), he is responsible for designing the university's academic calendar and independently designs, develops, deploys, and manages the SCFC (Student Centric Flexible Curriculum) LMS — a comprehensive learning management system in active use at the university, with requirements gathered and refined in collaboration with the Vice Chancellor of Amity University Bengaluru, following an Agile/DevOps approach. Built from the ground up with Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL, the system spans 24 modules and 160+ REST API endpoints covering timetable management, course registration, faculty allocation, attendance, marks, on-duty tracking, and reporting. It features a sophisticated multi-constraint conflict detection engine for scheduling across faculty, venues, and student groups, dual JWT-based authentication for staff and students, and is optimized to serve 2,000+ concurrent users with connection pooling tuned for high availability.

His research focuses on IoT interoperability, RESTful service architectures, Generative AI, Cloud, DevOps, and full-stack web development. He has published in Elsevier, Springer, and IEEE proceedings, and presented at IEEE DCOSS (Washington, D.C.), IMECS (Hong Kong), and IEEE CONECCT (Bangalore). He is proficient in Python, JavaScript, C, Java, React, Next.js, Django, and Node.js, and experienced with IoT platforms including STM32, Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi. He is a life member of ISTE and IETE, a member of IAENG, and winner of the Holistic Webdev Challenge and the Great Indian HP Code-Off.
